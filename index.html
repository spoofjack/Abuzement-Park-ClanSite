Suggestions:

  • Recruit new members! The team needs some fresh blood.

Members with a score below 0 will be recommended for kicking or demotion.

Members inactive for 3 days will be kicked.

A member who achieves 300 points is elegible for promotion to Elder at the discretion of leadership. Members must be in the clan for a at least 15 days to be promoted.

Score explanation:

Score is made of two components: donation score and war participation score.

Donation Score:

You are expected to donate 12 cards per day, on average. If you make more, you will gain points. If you make fewer, you will lose points. If you have zero, you will be penalized -40 points

War Participation Score:

For each of the last 10 wars, did member...: Yes: No:
...participate in the war? +5 -5
...complete each collection battle? (per battle) +5 -5
...win each collection battle? (per battle) +2 +0
...complete war day battle? +15 -30
...win war day battle? (per battle) +10 -5
